Our chatbot development process

  • 1

    Inception

    We work with you to learn about your goals and your business. We help you define the business objectives we will tackle and define the project scope.

  • 2

    Design & Development

    Next, our team of conversational design experts will create the conversation flows and integration points. Our development team then takes over and builds your chatbot.

  • 3

    Maintainance

    Finally, our team monitors your chatbot against real user conversations so we can continually optimze it for peak performance.
